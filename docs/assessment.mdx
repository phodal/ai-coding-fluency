# Self-Assessment Tool

import Assessment from '@site/src/components/FluencyModel/Assessment';

Use this interactive tool to assess your team's current fluency level across all five dimensions. The tool will:

1. **Guide you through each dimension** - Select your current level for each dimension
2. **Save your progress** - Your selections are automatically saved in your browser
3. **Provide recommendations** - Get specific next steps based on your current state

## How to Use

1. For each dimension, click the number (1-5) that best represents your team's current state
2. Review the level descriptions if you're unsure
3. Once all dimensions are selected, click "Show Recommendations" to see your personalized improvement path
4. Use "Reset Assessment" to start over

<Assessment />

## Understanding Your Results

Your assessment results show:

- **Current State**: Where your team is today across each dimension
- **Next Level**: The immediate next step for each dimension
- **Key Investments**: Specific actions needed to progress to the next level

## Tips for Accurate Assessment

- **Be honest**: Assess your actual current state, not where you want to be
- **Consider the whole team**: Evaluate based on team-wide capabilities, not individual outliers
- **Review regularly**: Reassess every 3-6 months to track progress
- **Focus on one dimension**: It's okay to be at different levels across dimensions
- **Incremental progress**: Moving up one level at a time is the recommended approach

## Next Steps

After completing your assessment:

1. **Share with your team** - Discuss the results and align on priorities
2. **Set realistic goals** - Choose 1-2 dimensions to focus on first
3. **Create an action plan** - Use the key investments as a starting point
4. **Track progress** - Set milestones and review regularly
5. **Iterate** - Adjust your approach based on what works

Remember: The goal isn't to reach the highest level in all dimensions immediately. Focus on sustainable, incremental improvements that align with your team's needs and capabilities.
